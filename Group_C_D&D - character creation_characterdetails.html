Here's more information on character details and backgrounds

Character Details

Details about your character define them

Physical attributes

Each character has their own personal look that sets them apart from the other adventurers. Your character's look involves physical features like height and weight and hair color and eye color. Clothes are also another way you can set your character apart. The important thing is that you give your character as many details as possible to keep them unique.

Personality Traits

Other important details about your character are their non physical traits. Your character's fears and aspirations are critical in keeping them an individual. All of the little likes and dislikes make your character more personal to you and help with the roleplaying experience.

Background

More information on backgrounds


What are backgrounds

Backgrounds let everyone know where your character came from and what experiences they had before they started adventuring. Backgrounds aside from just adding flavor to your character, also give them additional gear and proficiencies.



Choosing a background

Choosing the right background will help give your character their own identitiy. There's predetermined backgrounds in the player's handbook but there's also instructions on how to pick your own background. Picking the right background will give your character flavor and set them apart.
