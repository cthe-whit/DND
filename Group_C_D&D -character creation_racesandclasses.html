Races and Classes


Here's a breakdown on Races and Classes

Races

Different races have different specialties

Each race excels at different things

When building your character, taking into account what you'd like your character to do may help you choose which race you'd like your character to be. Each race possesses stats and traits that lend it to be more suitable for particular roles.

Considering Roleplaying

While each race may be better suited to a particular role, that doesn't mean that you have to choose that race for that role. Choosing a race for your character that is inconsistent with the role that you're intending your character to perform can be a very defining aspect of your character.

Classes

Each class has its own role for the team

Class Roles

While other choices that you make help define your character's role in their team, the class that you choose for your character is essentially their role. Whether you want your character to charge down the frontline or support their team from the back with healing or powerful spells, that's mainly determined by the class that you choose for them.

Outside of combat

The role for your character isn't based solely on how they help the team in combat. Being able to persuade people, pick locks, or circumvent nature can be equally essential to the team. Click here for some more information on each class and what they can do inside or outside of combat.
