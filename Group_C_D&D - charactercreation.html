Ability Scores, modifiers, and proficiencies

Now we're going to determine the ability scores and calculate the modifiers and proficiencies


Ability Scores

Ability scores are the defining attributes of your character. They determine how good or bad your character will be with the skills dependent on them and what strengths and weaknesses your character possesses.





Modifiers

Modifiers are the bonuses or penalties that are calculated based on the value of each particular skill that your character possesses and help or hinder you when you perform actions dependant on those skills.





Proficiencies

Proficiencies are the equipment, skills, tools, and other characteristics that your character is competent of or is skilled with.

Character details and background

Next step is to determine your character's background, alignment, and defining characteristics





Character details

Each character has their own name, physical characteristics, interests, and other defining features that set them apart from the other adventurers and aren't necessarily determined by their race or class.



Background

It's important to consider what kind of person your character is, what personality traits do they have, what are their ideals or fears, and what they've went through to get to where they are today.

Choosing Equipment

Now we'll choose equipment and make some calculations

Preset Equipment

Each class has some options of preset equipment that are well balanced and ensure you have everything you need to start your adventure. Some backgrounds also provide your character with additional equipment as well.

Buying your own Equipment

An alternative to choosing from preset equipment, you can use your class to determine your starting gold and buy your own gear to start the game with.

Non combat equipment

Don't forget the usefulness of non combat equipment! Sometimes your adventurer might run into a situation where a simple shovel or extra torch could make all the difference in their ability to complete their mission.

Equipment and Stats

A big determining factor of choosing equipment are the stats that you'll get from your weapons and armor. The armor you choose will play a large factor in how often you get hit by attacks and the weapon you choose will play a large factor into how easily you can hit your enemies and how much damage you deal to them.

Leveling

You'll need to level-up your character if your group isn't starting at first level





Starting above first level

In some situations, like if you're joining an existing group you'll get the chance to create a character that is created as if they've been adventuring for a while before they join that party.



How to level

Leveling involves your character gaining experience which grants them additional features from their class, more hitpoints, and improving their ability scores.

Character Sheets

We highly recommend putting your character's information on a character sheet!



What are they?

Character sheets are the standard tool for granting a visual of all aspects of your character for easy reference. They contain all of the information about your character such as their skills, attributes, and equipment.





Why use them

The biggest benefit of a character sheet is the quick reference it provides when you need to know a specific detail of your character. They also neatly organize all of your character's information which comes in handy when it comes time to level-up or make any other changes.
