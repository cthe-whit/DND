Ability Scores and such


Here's a breakdown on Ability Scores, Modifiers, and Proficiencies

Ability Scores

Here's a breakdown of the ability scores

This table shows what the ability scores do



Determining your scores

When building your character, you'll have to determine your scores before you assign them. Traditionally you'll roll 4d6 and discard the lowest die six times and assign the scores as you prefer. There's also other methods like point buy or assigning predetermined scores depending on what your playgroup prefers.

Assigning your scores

Once you have your six scores calculated you'll need to decide where you want to put them. Each class relies on different skills to be successful. Your character might want more strength or dexterity for their physical attacks or more intelligence, charisma, or wisdom for their magical attacks,or even more constitution to take more damage themselves.

Modifiers

More information on modifiers


What they are

Modifiers are derivied from ability scores. They are a representation of how that character performs with that particular ability with a modifier of 0 being the normal average. You can consult the table to help you find the modifiers for each skill of your character.



What they do

Each of your character's skills are affected by the modifier attributed to them. Your modifiers are also used to help with your character's saves. Additionally, modifiers are used for attacks both physical and magical to help them hit or do damage.

Proficiencies

Here's a breakdown of proficiencies

Proficiency Bonus

The proficiency bonus represents your character's experience and is determined by your character's level. Your proficiency bonus is added to skills, saves, and attacks that your character is proficient in.

Determining Proficiency

While your proficiency bonus is determined by level, what your character is proficient in is determined by your class. Your class will tell you what saves, skills, and attacks that your character is proficient in and you'll add your bonus to them.
