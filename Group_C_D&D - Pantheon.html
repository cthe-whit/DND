Making a Pantheon

Choose the Environment you would like to build...
Article made by Cameron DeGeorge

Your Pantheon is Your Own. Your own Goddesses, Gods, Titans, Demi-Gods and So on. Keep in limited. Do not have 50 gods, well, you could, just not ideal for you or the player. 10-25 should be fine. Naming them is simple: Take the core aspect or aspects of your God/Goddess and put them into Google Translate and form a work out of it. Let’s use The God of Darkness, Nightmare, and Death. If you pop this into Google Translate and set the output language to Latin, you come out with this: deus mortis, tenebris, et sequuntur somnia. Now That is a lot, let’s break it down into the pure core aspects. If you just the three main words, Darkness, Nightmares, and Death you get this: mortem, tenebris, sequuntur somnia. Now when I see this, I like to think, what can I do to forge a name. I split the words. Mor-tene-nia making Mortenenia which sounds like more of a Goddess name, but we are making a God, we need something more menacing. Now I see this Mor-tenebris, Mortenebris sounds good. The key to this is, you have to pick what sounds good, rolls off the tongue, easy to remember. Then alternate from God, Goddess, God, Goddess, until you like the list you make.
