Why choose gold

Deciding to purchase your own gear can be a good alternative to using the default class gear. Buying your own loadout allows you more customization which can lead to better roleplaying and opting out on items that you're not interested in having.

However...

Opting to buy your own equipment should be something you do with caution. It can be easy to forget to take essential gear with you that you may find needing later. You should only purchase your own gear if you're comfortable with the character building process and getting the items that your character needs and is able to use.

Non combat equipment

Equipment for outside of battle is equally as important

Why it's important

Your character needs certain non combat equipment each day such as a bedroll to sleep in or rations to consume. Additionally your character may sometimes find himself on a mission where it would be beneficial to have a torch to light his way or a shovel to dig something up. There's many situations through an adventure where your character can really benefit from having the right tool to complete the task at hand.

Being prepared

It can be easiest to purchase gear directly before a quest, but it's usually best to have extra supplies on hand in case of the unexpected. Equipment does have weight but that one extra torch could make all the difference. The stats of your equipment is a big factor when deciding which gear you want

Armor

Choosing armor can be difficult. More protective armor means more expensive more weight and more restrictive to your movement.You'll have to take these things into consideration if your character relies on being particularly agile or if they're more in the frontline. Here's a link that'll help give more details on armor and show you the options available to you.

Weapons

Like armor, choosing the weapons for your character to carry can be equally troublesome. Weight and cost is less of a factor when choosing your weapons, the main conern is the amount of damage they do and what type of damage they do.
